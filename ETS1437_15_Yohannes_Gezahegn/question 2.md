2.Miles on a Full Tank Calculator
Algorithm
Step 1: Start
Step 2: Read Fuel_Tank_Capacity
Step 3: Read Fuel_Consumption
Step 4: Compute Miles_on_Full_Tank = Fuel_Consumption*Fuel_Tank_Capacity
Step 5: Print Miles_on_Full_Tank
Step 6: Stop
